Why African Data Matters

Generalization improves when training data reflects real global conditions.

Most datasets are still concentrated in US and European environments. StreetPulse closes the representation gap with African urban context that improves robustness in production.

Overrepresented

US / Europe datasets

  • Wide-lane roads, strict signage systems
  • Predictable curb and parking behavior
  • Lower scene diversity in dense informal settings
Underrepresented

African urban environments

  • Mixed traffic modes and motorcycle-heavy mobility
  • High-density markets, informal stops, unmarked crossings
  • Weather-linked road and visibility changes
Outcome

Better AI model generalization

  • Lower false positives in unfamiliar scenes
  • Stronger resilience to long-tail edge conditions
  • Higher transferability across emerging markets
