Flood risk data Africa for infrastructure, insurance, and resilience planning

StreetPulse flood risk data Africa services help enterprises and public institutions quantify exposure across transport, logistics, and urban infrastructure systems.

What this dataset includes

Insurers, reinsurance analytics teams, ministries, infrastructure operators, development programs, and disaster-response stakeholders rely on these datasets.

Use cases include underwriting segmentation, claims forecasting, flood-prone corridor mapping, emergency routing plans, and resilience capex prioritization.

Schema may include flood_probability_band, inundation_proxy_depth, drainage_obstruction_tag, road_closure_likelihood, event_history_index, exposure_class, and confidence_interval.

StreetPulse provides map layers, tabular risk registers, API feeds, and executive-ready summaries for portfolio and operational decision-making.

Packages include assumptions register, model limitations, source provenance, and version controls for compliance teams.
