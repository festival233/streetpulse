Kampala, Uganda: Road Condition Dataset for Road Surface QA for Parish-to-Market Access

This city-level package provides surface quality, pothole density, rutting, and drivability indicators to support procurement-ready analytics and AI workflows for teams operating in Kampala.

Dataset description

StreetPulse curates Kampala-specific road condition records with quality controls, timestamped refresh logic, and operational metadata so teams can deploy quickly without rebuilding baseline data pipelines.

Coverage can be configured by corridor, district, floodplain, or sensing zone depending on your deployment scope and decision cycle.

Real-world use cases in Kampala

  • Public works teams prioritize resurfacing budgets using segment-level condition scores.
  • Fleet operators reduce vehicle wear by routing around high-damage corridors.
  • Insurers calibrate claims exposure with measurable road-surface risk attributes.
