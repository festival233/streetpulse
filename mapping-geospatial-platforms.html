Why StreetPulse is useful for this buyer

We combine field realism, commercial clarity, and enterprise delivery standards in one operating model. Buyers get location-specific African datasets with documented schema, QA traceability, and onboarding support for procurement and technical teams.

Specific dataset and service needs we support

Programs can include AI training data, computer vision annotation, traffic intelligence, road condition monitoring, flood exposure insights, environmental sensing, geospatial layers, and infrastructure risk scoring. Datasets are scoped to buyer objectives rather than generic catalogs.

Procurement and pilot workflow

We start with scope alignment, propose a pilot package, and finalize success metrics before full-scale rollout. Procurement artifacts include licensing options, metadata definitions, sample payloads, and support terms.

Typical deliverables
