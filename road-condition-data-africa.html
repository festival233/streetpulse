Road condition data Africa teams use for maintenance and risk control

StreetPulse road condition data Africa products transform fragmented field observations into structured intelligence for infrastructure and operations teams.

What this dataset includes

Typical buyers include ministries, concession operators, insurers, logistics companies, and infrastructure funds managing road-dependent portfolios.

Common uses include maintenance prioritization, contractor monitoring, underwriting support, corridor resilience scoring, and asset-level risk disclosure.

Example fields: segment_id, pavement_distress_type, pothole_density, surface_roughness_class, drainage_status, shoulder_integrity, hazard_severity, remediation_priority, and review_timestamp.

Delivery includes GIS layers, tabular condition inventories, image-linked evidence packs, and periodic refresh updates aligned to operating calendars.

Each release includes coverage notes, methodology summary, classification confidence, and data dictionary entries for auditability.
