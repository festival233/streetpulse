Traffic dataset Africa buyers use for operations, planning, and AI

StreetPulse traffic dataset Africa packages give enterprise buyers a reliable view of traffic behavior across complex and fast-changing transport networks.

What this dataset includes

Logistics platforms, mobility providers, urban operations centers, ministries, insurers, and infrastructure investors use these datasets to reduce uncertainty.

Use cases include delivery ETA optimization, congestion pricing analysis, road investment prioritization, fleet route planning, claims pattern detection, and scenario testing.

Schema examples include segment_id, speed_percentile_p50, speed_percentile_p95, congestion_duration_minutes, incident_frequency, vehicle_mix_proxy, demand_window, and confidence_score.

StreetPulse offers recurring batch snapshots, dashboard-ready extracts, and API endpoints with timestamped historical and near-real-time traffic intelligence feeds.

Metadata includes sampling strategy, coverage boundaries, freshness cadence, and quality thresholds so analysts can interpret limitations correctly.
